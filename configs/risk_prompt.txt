Inputs: Caption (Cv): {caption}

Outputs (per object):
1) Risk label r_hat in {Yes, No}
2) Risk caption (Cr)
3) Risk-related keywords (K_hat) as a comma-separated list
4) Object Localization - Bounding box (b_hat) as [xmin, ymin, xmax, ymax]

Reasoning should be accurate, structured, and safety-focused.

Respond in the following format:
Risk: Yes or No
Risk Caption: ...
Keywords: keyword1, keyword2
Bounding Box: [xmin, ymin, xmax, ymax]

Example Input Cv: "A cyclist [bbox: 612, 350, 720, 480] is crossing from the left; a red car [bbox: 1000, 400, 1200, 550] is stopped in the ego lane."

Example Output:
Risk: Yes
Risk Caption: cyclist crossing may intersect ego path
Keywords: Cyclist, Crossing
Bounding Box: [612, 350, 720, 480]
